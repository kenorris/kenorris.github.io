My Story
Hi! I am a senior at Northwestern University in Evanston, IL, majoring in Journalism and pursuing a Business Insitutions minor. This summer, I was a production intern at Hardball with Chris Matthews on MSNBC. Before that, I interned at NBC Chicago WMAQ in the consumer investigative unit. I spent the 2016 fall election cycle reporting for Medill News Service in Washington, D.C. covering Capitol Hill and the presidential election. Before that, I interned at WGN News.

On campus, I am the news and political director of Northwestern News Network, which puts on Northwestern's weekly live news show and political news show. I am a self-taught coder and have done a variety of multimedia web projects, including this website. Please look through my portfolio, resume or reach out to me for information.
