KELLY NORRIS

Hi! I am a junior at Northwestern University in Evanston, IL, majoring in Journalism and pursuing a Business Insitutions minor. Currently, I am the Health and Science reporter at Medill News Service in Washington, D.C. covering Capitol Hill and the Presidential Election. Previously, I was an Evening News Intern at WGN News.

When I am on campus, I am the Producer of the Northwestern News Network, Northwestern's weekly live news show, and work on Politicat, Northwestern's bi-weekly political news show. Additionally, I serve as the Vice President of Public Relations & Marketing of my sorority, Alpha Chi Omega.
