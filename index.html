Welcome! I am a Researcher with NBC Nightly News with Lester Holt in New York. I graduated from Northwestern University in January 2018 and have held a wide variety of internships across national and local news outlets. I am a self-taught coder and have completed a variety of multimedia web projects, including this website. Look through my portfolio, resume or reach out to me for information.
My Story
As a young child, I would wake up early to watch the Today Show. Every morning, I tried to learn about wars around the world, the nation’s politics and the latest pop culture before going to elementary school. As I grew older and actually began to understand what I was watching, I fell in love with news. From my first broadcasting class in 8th grade to now, I’ve grown as a journalist, producer and storyteller. I’ve won professional and student awards for my reporting and producing but the greatest privilege is getting to meet inspiring new people that are at the center each story.
