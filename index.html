My Story
As a young child, I would wake up early to watch the Today Show. Every morning, I tried to learn about wars around the world, the nation’s politics and the latest pop culture before going to elementary school. On weekends, I fell in love with journalistic storytelling while watching 60 Minutes every Sunday. From my first broadcasting class in 8th grade to now, I’ve grown as a journalist, producer and storyteller. I’ve won professional and student awards for my reporting and producing but the greatest privilege is getting to meet inspiring new people that are at the center each story.
